About Spardha 3.0

SPARDA 3.0, hosted by City Engineering College, is a vibrant multi-disciplinary college event featuring both technical and non-technical competitions, designed to showcase student talent, creativity, and teamwork across various domains. SPARDA encourages participation from all colleges, fostering national and regional collaboration.
